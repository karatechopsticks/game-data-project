Lego Ninjago: Shadow of Ronin is an action-adventure game for the Nintendo 3DS and PlayStation Vita. It is a sequel to the 2014 Lego Ninjago: Nindroids.[1] The game was released on 27 March 2015.[2]

Gameplay[edit]

Players will be able to control Kai, Zane, Cole, Jay, Lloyd, and more in their quest to defeat Ronin. It has been announced that players will be able to perform the Tornado of Creation to perform special elemental builds.[3]

Plot[edit]

After Garmadon sacrificed himself and was sent into the Cursed Realm,Lloyd left the team for a while to cope with losing his father. Kai, Zane, Cole and Jay, meet a new threat: Ronin, a mercenary who only seeks money. His hunt for riches leads him to steal the four ninjas' powers and memories. The ninja must now learn how to use their powers once again,by finding the Obsidian Weapons before Ronin uses an ancient evil to change Ninjago forever.
