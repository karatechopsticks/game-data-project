Toy Soldiers: War Chest is an upcoming video game developed by Signal Studios and published by Ubisoft.[2][3] The game will be released on Windows, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One in 2015.[4][5][6]

War Chest will be the first title in the series not to be a Microsoft console exclusive.[7]
