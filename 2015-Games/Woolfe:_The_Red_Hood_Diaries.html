Woolfe - The Red Hood Diaries is a platform video game by GRIN Gamestudio (not to be confused with the defunct Swedish video game developer GRIN). Woolfe is a cinematic fable featuring a vengeful Red Riding Hood. All grown up, armed with an axe, she sets out to take revenge on B.B. Woolfe and save her parents. The game was partially financed thanks to the Flanders Audiovisual Fund (VAF) which gave 60,000 EUR[1] and a Kickstarter campaign which gathered 72,139 USD.[2] The game was released on Microsoft Windows on March 17, 2015,[3] while the PlayStation 4 and Xbox One versions should follow the next quarter of 2015.[4]

Reception[edit]

Woolfe met with largely mixed or average reviews from critics, earning a 58 out of 100 average on Metacritic.[5] Some critics complained on how short the game was for the asking price of $9.99.[6] On Steam the game achieved a Mostly Positive from player reviews.[7]
