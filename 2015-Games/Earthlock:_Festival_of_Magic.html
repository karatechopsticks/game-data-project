Earthlock: Festival of Magic is an upcoming role-playing video game scheduled for release in the first half of 2015. It is scheduled for release for the Microsoft Windows, OS X and Linux computers first, and then Wii U, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One platforms soon after.[1]

Gameplay[edit]

The game will play as a non-linear, turn based role-playing video game.[2]

Story[edit]

The game's story takes place in the world of Umbra, where a cataclysmic event occurs that stops the planet from spinning.[3]

Development[edit]

Development for the game started as early as 2011,[4] with a pre-alpha build trailer being released in May 2013.[5] The game was originally just known by its subtitle, Festival of Magic.[6] In November 2013, to help funding, the game was put on to crowd sourcing platform Kickstarter.[7] However, Snowcastle later pulled from the platform, due to it getting lost in the crowd during the holiday season.[4] It was reactivated in March 2014.[4] The game's plot will be written by comic book writer Magnus Aspli.[8]

An early concept demo was released in March 2014 for the Windows, OS X and Linux platforms.[9]
