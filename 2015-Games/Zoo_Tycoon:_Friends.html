Zoo Tycoon Friends is an upcoming free-to-play business simulation developed by Canadian studio Behaviour Interactive and published by Microsoft Studios.[1]

Gameplay[edit]

Zoo Tycoon Friends will include over 40 unique species for players to adopt and care for. The zoo itself can be planned out, with players able to construct habitats for animals as well as concession stands, roads and decorations to keep visitors happy.

The player will be encouraged to feed, heal and play with animals to discover their unique personalities, as well as nurturing baby animals and helping them grow into adults. They can then visit their friends' zoos to help them care for their animals.[2]
