Digimon Story: Cyber Sleuth (デジモンストーリー　サイバースルゥース Dejimon Sutōrī: Saibā Surwūsu?) is a 2015 video game for the PlayStation Vita developed by Media.Vision and published by Bandai Namco Games, based on the Digimon franchise.[1]
