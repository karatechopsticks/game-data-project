Perimeter 2: New Earth

Developer(s) KDV Games
Publisher(s) Strategy First
Composer(s) Johnny Kratong
Series Perimeter
Platform(s) Microsoft Windows
Release date(s) February 13, 2009
Genre(s) Real-time strategy
Mode(s) Single-player, Multiplayer
Distribution DVD

Perimeter 2: New Earth (Russian: Периметр 2: Новая Земля) is a sequel to the 2004 real-time strategy video game Perimeter. Despite the commercial success of its predecessor, Perimeter 2 was not a big success.
