Shaun White Snowboarding: World Stage is a sports video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal for the Wii. It is the sequel to Shaun White Snowboarding and released on November 8, 2009.[1] Like the previous game, it is compatible with the Wii Balance Board, but the game can also be played with Wii MotionPlus.

Gameplay[edit]

There are 13 playable characters, including Shaun White. There are mountains in 8 different countries:

  •  Italy
  •  Canada
  •  Japan
  •  Chile
  •  France
  •  Great Britain
  •  USA
  •  Sweden

Development[edit]

The user interface (UI) of Shaun White Snowboarding: World Stage has been created with the middleware Menus Master by Omegame.[2]

Reception[edit]

Gametrailers gave the game 7.9 in their review, although said 'players should knock a point off if they can't play with the Wii Balance Board'.[3]
