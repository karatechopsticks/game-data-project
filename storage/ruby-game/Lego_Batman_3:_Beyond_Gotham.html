Lego Batman 3: Beyond Gotham is an upcoming 2014 Lego action-adventure video game developed by Traveller's Tales, for the PlayStation 4, PlayStation 3, PlayStation Vita, Nintendo 3DS, Wii U, Xbox One, Xbox 360, Microsoft Windows, and iOS. It is the third Lego Batman video game and the sequel to Lego Batman 2: DC Super Heroes. The game will be released in Europe on November 14 and in America on November 11, 2014.[1]

Premise[edit]

Following the events of Lego Batman 2: DC Super Heroes, Brainiac uses the powers of the Rings of each Lantern Corps to take over the universe by shrinking planets and cities to add to his collection. In pursuit of the Legion of Doom, Batman and the Justice League must travel into the far reaches of outer space to stop them.[2]

Downloadable content[edit]

On September 9, 2014, WB Games announced of a season pass that will consist of Downloadable Content of levels & characters currently based after Man of Steel, Christopher Nolan's Dark Knight Trilogy and a 75th anniversary storyline.[11] the Season Pass will be available for the PS3, PS4, Xbox One, Xbox 360 and PC, no word has been given if the Season Pass or any DLC will be available for the Wii U version. On September 24, 2014, it was announced that an Amazon UK exclusive that a pre-order bonus would feature a "Rainbow Raider" and "Rainbow Batman" character pack and a Batman Beyond pack only for the Playstation 3 & Playstation 4 at the initial release for free, then later on for other consoles for a fee. [13][4][42] At the 2014 New York Comic-Con, a DLC pack was announced that would include an additional level and several playable characters based on the television series Arrow.[43]
