Tenkai Knights: Brave Battle (テンカイナイト ブレイブバトル, Tenkai Naito Bureibu Batoru) is a fighting game developed and published by Bandai Namco Games, based on the anime series Tenkai Knights.[3] It is released for the Nintendo 3DS on September 25, 2014 in Japan and on October 7, 2014 in North America.

Development[edit]

The game was developed by Bandai Namco Games, and was originally announced in January 2014. In the game ,Guren, Ceylan, Toxsa and Chooki accidentally fell through an inter-dimensional portal. When they find themselves on the other side, the group are on another world and have assumed the mantles of Bravenwolf, Tributon, Valorn and Lydendor — otherwise known as the legendary Tenkai Knights. They fight evil to protect both dimensions.[4]

Gameplay[edit]

In Tenkai Knights: Brave Battles, the player builds and customizes robots and then engages in combat with other robots. The game draws inspiration from the Tenkai Knights animated series.
