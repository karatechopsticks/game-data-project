The Last Tinker: City of Colors is a platforming game developed by Mimimi Productions[1] for Microsoft Windows, OS X and Linux which was released on May 12, 2014.[2] The PlayStation 4 version was handled by Loot Entertainment, and released on August 19, 2014 in North America and August 20, 2014 in Europe.

An Xbox One version of the game was planned, but was cancelled due to poor sales on the PS4.[3]

Reception[edit]

Reception
Review scores

Matt Beaudette of Hardcore Gamer gave the game a 4/5, saying "The beautiful visuals, brilliantly realized art style, and enjoyable music will put a smile on even the most cynical faces." [5]

Mat Growcott of GamesReviews gave the game a 5.5/10, saying "For those that have played the latest Pac-Man TV series games and who want something to keep them going until the sequel is released: The Last Tinker is for you and for your children" [4]

Max Burton of The Freelance Critic gave the PC version of the game a 7/10, citing that 'it’s clear that the game was designed specifically for children' as well as complimenting the games visuals and simplistic gameplay. [6]
