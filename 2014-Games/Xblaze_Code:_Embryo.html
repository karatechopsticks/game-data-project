XBlaze Code: Embryo is a prequel visual novel of the fighting game series BlazBlue by Arc System Works. It was released in Japan for the PlayStation 3 and PlayStation Vita on July 23, 2013 and North America on June 24, 2014 by Aksys Games.[1]

A sequel titled Xblaze: Lost Memories has been announced for the PS3 and Vita, to be released in 2015.[2]

Plot[edit]

Set 50 years before the Dark War and 150 years before the events of Calamity Trigger, XBlaze Code: Embryo introduces Toya Kagari, a normal high school student who is introduced to the mysterious "Power of the Azure."

Reception[edit]

DualShockers gave the game a review score of 7.5 out of 10, praising the art and music, however mentions that the game features occasional glitches.[3]
