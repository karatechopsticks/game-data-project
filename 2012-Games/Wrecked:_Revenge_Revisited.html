Developer(s) Supersonic Software

Wrecked: Revenge Revisited (previously known as Gas: Fuel For Fun) is a vehicular combat racing video game developed by Supersonic Software and published by 505 Games for PlayStation Network and Xbox Live Arcade.

Reception[edit]

Wrecked: Revenge Revisited received mixed to negative reviews. Aggregating review websites GameRankings and Metacritic gave the PlayStation 3 version 54.00% and 64/100[1][2] and the Xbox 360 version 46.54% and 57/100.[3][4]
