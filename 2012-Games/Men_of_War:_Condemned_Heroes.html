Men of War: Condemned Heroes

Publisher(s) 1C Company
Series Men of War
Platform(s) Microsoft Windows
Release date(s) April 12, 2012
Genre(s) Real-time tactics / strategy
Mode(s) Single-player / Co-op / Multiplayer
Distribution download

Men of War: Condemned Heroes is a real-time tactics / strategy game set during World War II. It tells the story of Soviet penal battalions formed under Stalin's infamous Order 227.
