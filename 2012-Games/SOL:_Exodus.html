SOL: Exodus is a space combat simulator by Seamless Entertainment, an independent game developer located in Austin, Texas, released on January 25, 2012.[1]

Gameplay[edit]

The game is set in the solar system, and features both dogfighting and battles with capital ships.[2] The game is described by the designers as being in the spirit of older space combat simulators such as Wing Commander, FreeSpace, Descent and Star Wars: X-Wing.[2][3]
