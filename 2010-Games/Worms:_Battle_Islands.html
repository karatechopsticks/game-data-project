Worms: Battle Islands is an artillery strategy game developed by Team17 and part of the Worms series. It was released for the PlayStation Portable and Wii.

Gameplay[edit]

Like previous Worms games, Worms: Battle Islands is a turn-based strategy game, in which the player takes turns controlling worms, and firing weapons on destructible terrain.

Reception[edit]

GamesRadar rated the game 7/10, stating that while it may be the best Worms game yet, it hasn't changed much.[1]
