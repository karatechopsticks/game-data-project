Shaun White Skateboarding is a skateboarding game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and published by Ubisoft for Xbox 360, PlayStation 3, Wii, and Microsoft Windows,[1] released on October 24, 2010. The game is also available through OnLive.[2] It is the third title in the Shaun White series and will also be the first game published by Ubisoft to not ship with an instruction manual and instead use a digital manual.[3]

Despite having Shaun in the title and cover of the game, you can only play him near the end of the game, the rest of the time is the character you create in the beginning. Upon completion of the game, you gain the ability to freely play as Shaun White.

Soundtrack[edit]

The soundtrack of the game was released in 2010 and contains 55 songs by Charm City Devils, Green Day, Kiss, Matt & Kim, Metric, Motion City Soundtrack, NOFX, Tokyo Police Club, The Broken West, Weezer, Wolfmother and many others.[4]

Some of the songs that appear are: "Barcelona" by The Plasticines, "Disappear" by Motion City Soundtrack, "The Warning (Save Ourselves)" by The Blackout, "Stadium Love" by Metric, "Senioritis" by Red Jumpsuit Apparatus, "Your English Is Good" by Tokyo Police Club, and "Daylight" by Matt and Kim.
