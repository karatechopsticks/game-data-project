Ben 10: Alien Force-The Rise of Hex is the fourth game in the Ben 10 video game series, the third game to be based on the Ben 10: Alien Force series, and the first downloadable game based on the series. It was released on May 26, 2010 on the Xbox Live Arcade worldwide and released on the WiiWare in North America on May 31, 2010 and in the PAL region on December 17, 2010. It is no longer available after being pulled from both Wiiware and Xbox Live Arcade.
