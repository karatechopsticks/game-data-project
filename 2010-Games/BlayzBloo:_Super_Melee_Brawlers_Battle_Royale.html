BlayzBloo: Super Melee Brawlers Battle Royale, released in Japan as BlazBlue: Battle x Battle (ぶれいぶるー　-バトル×バトル-, BureiBurū: Batoru kurosu Batoru?) is a 3D arena fighting game developed by Arc System Works. It was published in Japan by Arc System Works on January 27, 2010 and for the Nintendo DSi/Nintendo DSi XL. The game was released in North America on August 2, 2010.

The game costs 500 Nintendo Points (5 dollars USD). This game recycles sounds, speech, and music from BlazBlue: Continuum Shift.

Characters[edit]

The game features five characters. Each character has one basic attack and a drive attack, as well as a description.

  • Ragna the Bloodedge
    • Soul Eater
    • Unwilling protagonist, wielder of the Azure Grimoire, and the strongest around. He absorbs his opponents' life with the power of darkness.
  • Jin Kisaragi
    • Reitou (Frost Bite)
    • A major in the NOL and afflicted with a rather unhealthy attachment to his brother, Ragna. He stops opponents in their tracks with his ice attacks.
  • Noel Vermillion
    • Chain Revolver
    • Ever the tragic heroine, she is easily driven to tears. Her Drive ability starts a powerful chain attack.
  • Taokaka
    • Dancing Edge
    • A girl in Kagutsuchi's Kaka clan. She speeds to victory with her dash attacks.
  • Rachel Alucard
    • Silpheed
    • Head of the Alucard vampire family. She manipulates the wind with the aid of Silpheed and her familiars. Her attacks are magnificent.

Modes[edit]

There are three modes.

  • Life (Beat-em Up!)
  • Point (Collect noodles)
  • Flag (Capture the panda flag)

Multiplayer[edit]

Players can play with other people who have downloaded the game to their DSi /DSi XL /3DS. There can be up to four people per match. Computers join if there are fewer than four players.
