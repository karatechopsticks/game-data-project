iCarly 2: iJoin the Click! is a video game loosely based on the TV series of the same name. It is the sequel to the original iCarly game.[2]

Gameplay[edit]

Players will play as a new student at Ridgeway who begins to hang out with Carly, Sam and Freddie at locations from the show, such as Groovy Smoothie, the Pacific Place Mall, and even Carly’s apartment. Gameplay is not the same as the original.

Reception[edit]

Much like the first iCarly game, it is received mostly negatively, criticized for its simple and repetitive gameplay.[citation needed]
