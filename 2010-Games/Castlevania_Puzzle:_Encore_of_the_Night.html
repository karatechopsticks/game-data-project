Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night is a Castlevania puzzle game featuring Alucard and other cast members of Castlevania: Symphony of the Night made for iOS.[1] It retells the story of the original Castlevania: Symphony of the Night. It features an RPG system and touched up sprites. The game was released on iOS on July 21, 2010 and for Windows Phone on January 18, 2011.[2][3][4]

Reception[edit]

The game received a 7.4/10 from IGN[5] and an A- from 1up.com.[6] Game Informer‍ '​s Tim Turi called it his "go-to puzzle game" on his iPhone in part due to its use of Symphony of the Night‍ '​s sprites.[7]
