Release date(s)
  • WW November 22, 2013
Genre(s) Fighting

Fighter Within is a fighting game developed by Daoka and published by Ubisoft. It was released as an exclusive launch title for the Xbox One.[1][2]

The game was panned by critics, and holds a Metacritic score of 23 out of 100.[3] The Kinect controls were universally criticized, with several reviews suggesting problems were just as prevalent as the Kinect for Xbox 360. GameInformer gave the game a 1 out of 10, saying "Fighter Within is garbage." [6][8]GameZone's Mike Splechta gave Fighter Within a 2.5/10, stating "Fighter Within really showcases the larger problem with Kinect only games: they just don't work."[9]
