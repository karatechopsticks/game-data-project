Zeno Clash II is a first-person action role-playing[2]-fighting video game developed by ACE Team as the follow-up to Zeno Clash, a sequel that Atlus published for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 in 2013.[3][4][5][6] Zeno Clash II features multiplayer game mode.[7]

Reception[edit]

Zeno Clash II was generally received less favourably by critics than its predecessor, with the PC version receiving a rating of 61/100 on reviews aggregator Metacritic.[8] The most favourable rating by nowgamer [9] says the game bests it's antecessor, praising the "sumptuous looking and stylish" world and "excellent" combat system but noting that Zeno Clash II is "l obviously not be for everyone, and its low budget does show through." Other critics praise the artwork and characters but cite various flaws and most seem to agree that the game fell behind in comparison to the first game. Destructoid said "Zeno Clash II might be bigger than its predecessor, but it fails to be truly better." [10]
