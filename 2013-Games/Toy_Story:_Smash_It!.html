Toy Story: Smash It! is an iOS and Android game developed by Disney Mobile Studios and released on February 28, 2013.[1] It is based on the Toy Story franchise.[2] The game was retired on August 29, 2014.[3]

Critical reception[edit]

The game has a Metacritic score of 84% based on 10 critic reviews.[4]
