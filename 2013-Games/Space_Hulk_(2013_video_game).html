Space Hulk (2013 video game)

Space Hulk
Developer(s) Full Control
Platform(s) Microsoft Windows, OS X, Linux, iOS
Genre(s) Turn-based tactics

Space Hulk is a turn-based tactics video game developed by Full Control and released on 15 August 2013.[1] It based on the tabletop game of the same name by Games Workshop.[1] It features the Blood Angels Chapter of the Space Marines battling grotesque aliens known as Genestealers.

Space Hulk has received mixed to average reviews. Review aggregator Metacritic has estimated an average score of 58 out of 100 based on 22 reviews, with most reviewers citing a lack of polish and a too stringent adherence to the tabletop game rules.[4]
